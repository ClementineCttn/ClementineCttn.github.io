Research Focus


I am interested in cities, in what happens within and between cities, with respect to demographic, economic and social processes. In my doctoral and post-doctoral research, I have used the framework of complex systems to analyse and model urban evolution and urbanisation in different spatial and temporal contexts (Europe, Russia, France, UK). In particular, I looked at inequality and the uneven distribution of city sizes, urban attributes and economic variables, and tried to examine the sensitivity of definitional choices on their evaluation, along with the potential causes and theoretical explanations for such inequality in general and in particular contexts.
